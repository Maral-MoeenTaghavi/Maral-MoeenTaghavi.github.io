Biography

After I got my B.S degree in Electrical Engineering I knew it wasn't meant to be my dream major! So I decided to change my career and become a pro artist!
I got my M.A. in Painting and after doing loads of tries and errors, practicing, and not giving up, I made my dreams come true...
Now I am honored to say that: I AM AN ANIMATOR :)
Later, I joined Hoorakhsh Studios, one of the pioneers in animation production in Iran, as an animator. I soon become promoted to animation supervisor and enjoyed working on different projects all around the world.
Today I live in Germany and dream to expand my experiences. I work on my animating skills every day and learn new ones such as lighting, concept art, and a whole new world of 3D design and animation.
